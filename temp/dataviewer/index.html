Exploring Your CO2

What's in the map?

The Renewable Energy Potential in Metro Vancouver

The Community Energy Explorer is a project by the Collaborative for Advanced Landscape Planning (CALP) at the University of British Columbia's Centre for Interactive Research on Sustainability (CIRs).

The map shows modeled potential sources of renewable energy throughout the Metro Vancouver region and is intended to facilitate discussion around exploring renewable energy and the related technologies.

The goal of the maps is to complement the educational resources in the Community Energy Explorer site and help make these data accessible to the public, practitioners, and policy makers.

Funding provided by Metro Vancouver, Neptis Foundation, Real Estate Foundation of BC, the Vancouver Foundation, and Pacific Institute for Climate Solutions (PICS).

How to use the maps

  1. Add layers by clicking the icons under the "Add Layers" dropdown menu.
  2. Hover over the data with your cursor to reveal the specific data values in the infobox (bottom left).
  3. Hover over the "Legend" text to see the layer legends.
  4. Expand/Close the box on the bottom of the page to reveal more specific information about each of the datasets. Click on the tabs to get information for each of the different layers.
  5. Have fun, learn, and make a change!
